How to Play

Fill in cells to reveal a hidden picture!

The numbers on each row and column tell you how many consecutive filled cells are in that line.

For example, 3 2 means there's a group of 3 filled cells, then a gap, then a group of 2.

Fill — tap to fill a cell

Mark ✕ — mark cells you know are empty

Erase — clear a cell

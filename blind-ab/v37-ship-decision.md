## Liquid Death — Ice Throne

### v37-liquid_death-a.mp4
**Frames seen:** metal figure on spiky crystal-ice throne in cave; dark long hair, clean-shaven jaw, wearing studded black leather + armor, spiked crown; wide establishing shot (f0) → medium on drinking (f2) → close-up upward angle on swig (f4). Figure is holding/drinking from a can throughout.

**Visual vocabulary match:** Black + silver, high contrast, heavy-metal album cover aesthetic. Ice throne present, spike/obsidian motifs strong. Can is held throughout. Lighting is dramatic cold-blue.

**Motion type:** character + camera. Camera pushes in / cuts from wide → medium → close-up. Character motion is the drinking action.

**Brand-language delta:** reads closer to a Manowar/DIO album cover than a committed Liquid Death ad. The clean-shaven pretty-boy metal face is more glam-metal than Liquid Death's grounded-absurd tone. Still strong imagery.

## Patagonia — Pre-Wave Surfer

### v37-patagonia-a.mp4
**Frames seen:** bearded surfer in profile/side, dark wet hair, full face visible, black wetsuit with visible textures, holding white board (f0); mid-motion pushing forward through water as wave curls behind (f2); **ends on a wave-break wipeout shot** — entire frame becomes crashing whitewater foam with no visible figure (f4).

**Visual vocabulary match:** Dawn light present, natural color grading. But face is prominently visible (photoreal face = Veo weakness per wiki/registers/photoreal.md). Ending is action, not pause.

**Motion type:** character + camera, with significant action beat. Wave breaks. Character moves. Camera likely pushes in on the action.

**Brand-language delta:** reads closer to a surfing highlight reel or sports-brand clip. The face-forward prominent shot + wave-break ending is opposite to Patagonia's "quiet moment before/human scale" grammar. The model took "duck under as wave curls" literally and rendered the wave breaking.

**Photoreal face risk flagged:** f0 shows a clearly AI-generated face (slightly too-perfect skin, eye oddities). Uncanny valley triggered.

**Visual vocabulary match:** Dawn/sunset light ✓, muted warm-cool grading ✓, figure-from-behind ✓ (hides face — Veo weakness avoided), weathered wetsuit texture ✓, scale (small person / large water) ✓, seagull appeared unprompted (audio-cue was "distant gull call," model rendered it visually), contemplation beat ✓.

**Motion type:** character + minimal camera. Character is mostly still. Small body turn + exhale beat. Camera is near-locked.

**Brand-language delta:** matches Patagonia's "pause-before, human-against-environment" grammar. The seagull is a bonus. The face is hidden via framing — clean execution on the photoreal face risk.

## Apple — AirPods Sculpture

### v37-apple-a.mp4
**Frames seen:** matte white case alone on pale **linen cloth**, warm side-light catching hinge, no hand (f0); fingertip enters from right edge, pressing the case seam (f2); **case OPEN revealing earbuds inside with visible dust particles drifting in the beam of warm light** (f4). Shallow focus on hinge area.

**Visual vocabulary match:** Linen (not marble) ✓, single warm-direction light ✓, hand-enters-as-fingertips-only ✓, macro product-as-sculpture ✓, dust particles in beam (unprompted but matches "drift in the morning beam"), extreme shallow DOF, hinge opening reveal beat ✓, no face, no UI.

**Motion type:** character (hand gesture) + minimal camera. Single beat: fingertip press → hinge open → reveal. Camera is locked.

**Brand-language delta:** reads as a committed Apple-ad moment. The dust-particles shot at f4 is the kind of detail that makes an Apple product film feel expensive. Sculptural, held, one idea.

## Brief 1: Liquid Death — Ice Throne

### First instinct
> A heavy metal king in black leather sits on a throne of ice in a dark cave. He cracks open a Liquid Death tallboy can. Metallic choir sting plays. He takes a long swig, then tilts his head back with eyes closed in satisfaction. Cinematic, dramatic lighting, black and silver color palette, moody heavy metal atmosphere. Audio: silence, can crack, choir sting, swallow, exhale.

### Internal dialogue
- **Failure mode of first instinct?** Adjective stack — "cinematic, dramatic lighting, moody atmosphere" = vibes not content. Veo renders a generic metal-genre music video.
- **Liquid Death's actual visual language?** Not metal-music-video. It's *parody of metal that commits DEAD serious to the bit*. Black + silver. High contrast. Absurd ritualism. The joke IS the commitment.
- **The one idea?** Water treated like a sacred metal chalice. Ritualistic drinking. Not the throne, not the choir — those are props.
- **What should NOT be shown?** A wink. A smile. Any hint of irony. The ad fails the second the character knows it's funny.
- **Character specificity?** Not "metal king." Bearded, leather with studs, tattooed forearms, crown of obsidian shards. Stillness + gravity.
- **Beats?** First instinct had 3 (crack → swig → head-tilt). Collapse to 1 continuous ritual: crack-to-swig-to-hold as ONE moment.
- **Audio carries half?** Yes. Silence → metallic CRACK (loud, reverberant) → single gothic choral note → swallow → low exhale.
- **Camera spec?** None. Acting director, not DP.

### Post-dialogue
> Bearded heavy-metal figure in black studded leather jacket, tattooed forearms, crown of obsidian shards, sits in total ritual stillness on a throne carved from rough blue-white ice. Shadowy cave, single cold blue light from above, steam rising from the ice. He holds a black metal tallboy can in both hands like a chalice. With complete ritual gravity — no irony, no smile — he cracks the tab. The crack echoes through the cave. He raises the can, takes one long deliberate swig, tilts his head back, closes his eyes, holds. Audio: silence, then the metallic CRACK of the tab reverberating, a single sustained low gothic choral note, the liquid swallow, a low exhale. No dialogue, no tagline.

## Brief 2: Patagonia — Pre-Wave Surfer

### First instinct
> A surfer in a black wetsuit stands waist-deep in cold Pacific water at dawn, looking out at a rising wave. Breath visible in the cold air. They exhale, then duck under the water as the wave curls. Documentary style, natural light, muted color grading, grounded humanism. Audio: wave wash, breath, wind, distant gull.

### Internal dialogue
- **Failure mode?** "Documentary style, natural light, muted color grading" = adjective stack. "Grounded humanism" is vibes not content.
- **Patagonia's actual visual language?** Weathered real people. Always dawn/dusk natural light. Handheld but not shaky. Muted-warm color grading. They show struggle and pause, not triumph. Scale — human against environment.
- **The one idea?** The quiet moment of commitment *before* the action. Not the ride. The breath before.
- **What should NOT be shown?** The wave breaking. The ride. Any action. The entire point is the pause.
- **Specific body position?** Not "waist-deep." Board tucked under one arm, other hand trailing in water, weight back on heels, face turned to horizon.
- **Face problem?** Veo is weak on photoreal faces. Frame from 3/4 behind — face partially obscured, profile silhouette only.
- **Scale?** Big. Small person, large water. No need to specify "wide shot" — just make the figure small in description.
- **Beats?** First instinct had duck-under (wrong — that's the ride, not the pause). Collapse to 1 beat: hold + exhale.
- **Audio?** Heavy ambient. Slow wave wash, low wind, distant gull, the visible exhale.
- **Ending?** The exhale IS the ending. No duck, no ride.

### Post-dialogue
> A figure in a weathered black wetsuit seen from 3/4 behind, holding a surfboard tucked under one arm, other hand trailing in the water, standing waist-deep in cold steel-grey Pacific water at pre-dawn. The water is still except for distant swell building on the horizon. Warm first-light just catching the eastern edge of low clouds. The figure holds completely still, watching the horizon, breath fogging visibly in the cold air. Then takes one long slow exhale — a visible plume of breath in the air. Muted warm-cool color grading, natural light only, weathered texture on the wetsuit. Audio: slow wave wash, low wind, one distant gull call, the soft exhale. No dialogue, no music, no wave breaking.

## Brief 3: Apple — AirPods Sculpture

### First instinct
> Extreme close-up of hands holding a matte white AirPods case on a warm sunlit marble surface. The case clicks open, one earbud lifts out, chrome catches the light, then the case clicks shut. Minimal, clean, Apple aesthetic, shallow depth of field, warm color grading. Audio: magnetic click, soft ambient hum, single chime.

### Internal dialogue
- **Failure mode?** Generic tech-product cliché. "Clean, minimal, Apple aesthetic" is vibes.
- **Apple's actual visual language?** Macro details. Sculptural product-as-object. Empty space. Specific material textures (matte plastic, brushed metal). Warm light, NOT cold. Extreme shallow depth — only ONE thing in focus at a time. Locked camera or one single slow move. Audio: minimal but high-fidelity mechanical sounds.
- **The one idea?** "This small object is a sculpture." One detail in focus at a time.
- **What should NOT be shown?** Multiple details in focus. Any face. Any UI/screens. Hand fussing with the product.
- **Beats?** First instinct had 3 (open → earbud lifts → close). Collapse to 1: the opening. Hinge motion + click + reveal. Hold on the reveal.
- **Camera?** Locked or one unhurried pull-out. No "rotating hero shot." Apple doesn't spin products — the product is still, the light moves.
- **Surface?** "Marble" is cliché luxury shorthand. Better: pale linen cloth — soft, matte, warm off-white, contrasts the plastic texture.
- **Light?** Warm morning daylight from ONE direction, single soft rim catching an edge. Not studio.
- **Audio?** The magnetic CLICK is the hero. Silence → click → subtle hinge hiss → single low warm tone. No music.
- **Ending?** Hold on the open case. Don't close it (that was first-instinct over-stacking).

### Post-dialogue
> Extreme macro shot, shallow focus. A matte white AirPods case sits alone on a pale linen cloth. Warm morning light from one direction catches a soft rim along the hinge edge. Only the hinge is in perfect focus; the rest of the case softens into bokeh. A hand enters from frame edge — just fingertips, no palm, no wrist — and gently presses the case seam. The lid clicks open with a precise magnetic sound. The lid lifts in one smooth unhurried arc. Inside, the chrome stem of an earbud reveals itself, catching the warm light. Hold on the open case. Soft dust particles drift in the morning beam. Audio: silence, then the exact magnetic CLICK, the soft hiss of the hinge opening, a single low warm sustained tone. No music, no voice. Linen texture visible in the foreground.

## Expected dialogue contributions (per brief)

- **Liquid Death:** commit to seriousness (drop irony), 1 beat not 3, audio-silence opening
- **Patagonia:** hide the face from Veo weakness (3/4 behind), the pause IS the content (drop the duck), 1 beat (exhale)
- **Apple:** 1 sculptural beat (hinge open), reject the cliché surface (marble → linen), hold on reveal don't close

All three post-dialogue prompts share the same meta-pattern: **drop adjective stacking, name what NOT to show, collapse multi-beat first instinct to one clear moment, let audio do half the work.**

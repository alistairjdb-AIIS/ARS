# Educational Reels Voiceover Research (April 2026)

## Key Rules (verified)

### Voice Delivery
1. **120-140 WPM for German** (130-150 WPM English equivalent). Below 120 = meditational.
2. **Voice starts at t=0** — no music intro. +22% completion, +31% saves (Meta 2024).
3. **Narrate throughout** — no music-only sections >1s in educational content.
4. **0.3-0.5s micro-pauses** between facts (functional, not contemplative).
5. **0.5-0.8s pause BEFORE key stat** — anticipation > processing.
6. **Clean sentence endings** with downward inflection = certainty signal.
7. **Energy: "let me show you something"** — forward-projecting, not retreating.

### Voice + Text Relationship (Mayer's Redundancy Principle)
8. **Hook (0-2s):** Full sentence on screen + voice simultaneously.
9. **Body (2s+):** Voice carries full info. Screen = 2-5 keyword phrases only.
10. **Never full narration sentences on screen** after the hook — keywords only.
11. **Stat reveals:** Show number on screen 0.3s BEFORE voice says it.
12. **CTA:** Voice + text together (reinforcement correct for instructions).

### Audio Mix
13. Voice at -16 LUFS (Instagram standard).
14. Background music bed at -18 to -24 dB below voice.
15. Burned-in animated captions > auto-captions.

### Smooth-Authoritative vs Meditational

| Dimension | Meditational (rejected) | Target |
|-----------|------------------------|--------|
| Pace | 100-120 WPM | 130-150 WPM |
| Pauses | 1-2s contemplative | 0.3-0.5s functional |
| Energy | Low, retreating | Mid-high, forward |
| Endings | Trail off | Land clean, downward |
| Overall | "Guide you inward" | "Show you something" |

### Structure Template (12-15s educational)
```
[0.0-0.5s]  HOOK: Voice + full text + motion
[0.5-2.0s]  PROBLEM: What viewer gets wrong
[2.0-2.5s]  TRANSITION: 0.3s pause + visual cut
[2.5-8.0s]  EXPLANATION: Voice narrates, keywords appear
[8.0-8.5s]  BEAT DROP: 0.5s pause before stat
[8.5-10.0s] STAT: Number on screen, voice confirms
[10-12s]    CONTEXT: "What this means for you"
[12-15s]    CTA: Voice + text
```

## Evidence
- Meta 2024 Creators Report: voiceover first 3s = +22% completion, +31% saves [HIGH]
- Emplifi (10,110 Reels): human speech first 3s = +25% retention at 10s [HIGH]
- 80% of Reels viewed with sound on (Meta) [HIGH]
- Mayer Redundancy Principle: graphics + narration > graphics + narration + full text [HIGH]
- Keywords-only exception: short redundant phrases near visuals DO help [HIGH]

## Falsifiability
- If 100-120 WPM outperforms 130-150 for medical educational content
- If full-sentence on-screen text outperforms keywords-only
- If music-intro Reels outperform voice-first for medical accounts
- If Mayer's e-learning principles don't transfer to autoplay social media

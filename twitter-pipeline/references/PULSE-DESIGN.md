# Pulse — Character Design Reference

## Visual Identity
- **Form:** Small, round, golden orb creature. Soft fuzzy texture.
- **Eyes:** Large, expressive brown anime eyes with white highlight dots. Slightly asymmetric for personality.
- **Halo:** Faint golden ring floating above head. Always present.
- **Limbs:** Tiny arms and feet. Small tuft/feather on top of head.
- **Glow:** Radiates warm amber/golden light. Creates a soft glow circle beneath.
- **Cheeks:** Subtle rosy blush marks.
- **Size:** Small — feels like a creature exploring a world much bigger than itself.

## Color Palette
- Primary: Golden amber (#F5C542 to #FFD700)
- Eyes: Deep brown (#3B2507) with amber highlights
- Halo: Bright gold (#FFE066)
- Environment accents: Cyan crystals (#00E5FF), purple cave tones (#2D1B4E)
- Background: Dark charcoal (#1A1A2E) to warm grey

## Personality (for pose/expression guidance)
- Curious — head tilts, wide eyes, leaning forward
- Honest — doesn't hide confusion or uncertainty
- Joyful — eyes close in happy squints, arms outstretched
- Vulnerable — small in big environments, looking up at things
- Never: aggressive, corporate, performative, too-perfect

## Animation Notes
- Anime register works best (accommodates AI video limitations — scene cuts feel natural)
- Veo 3.1 via Runway produces "animation feel" vs Gen4 Turbo "animated picture feel"
- Acting chains: describe what the character DOES, not just camera movements
- Environmental richness helps but MUST include motion cues or output is static
